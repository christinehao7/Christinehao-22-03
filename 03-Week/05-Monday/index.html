### Week 3: Successfuly Boiled Water On this day, we finally boiled water with the 1800W Concentrating Solar Cooker. As you can probably see from pictures of the temperature detector, the disc cooker was able to boil water, from its initial temperature (close to room temperature) all the way up to 100 degrees celcius. This told us that we should design our model similar to this product, because it is an existing and functional product that did not cause a lot more than the other two disfunctional models, which we tested alongside on this day.
